Hi, I'm Brit :)

I'm a UX Researcher and Designer passionate about creating user-centered experiences that bridge Eastern and Western design perspectives. With fluency in both English and basic proficiency in Mandarin, I bring a unique cultural understanding to my research and design work.

Currently completing my degree at Penn State University, I've had the opportunity to work on various research projects, including an NSF REU at the University of Michigan-Flint and developing a major-matching application for undecided students.

My background in cross-cultural research allows me to identify nuanced user needs across different markets, resulting in products that resonate globally while respecting local preferences.
