About Me

As a bilingual UX researcher fluent in English and Mandarin, I bring a unique cross-cultural perspective to every project. With 5 years of experience, I specialize in conducting user research that bridges Eastern and Western design philosophies, creating products that resonate with diverse user bases.

My background in cognitive psychology and human-computer interaction, combined with my multicultural understanding, allows me to uncover deep insights that might be missed in traditional single-culture research approaches.

I'm passionate about creating inclusive, culturally sensitive designs that can succeed in both global and local markets. My goal is to help companies create products that not only meet user needs but also respect and celebrate cultural differences.
